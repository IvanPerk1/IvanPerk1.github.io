How to set up Mac Agent

1
Download & Install

Download the .dmg file, open it, and drag Wake PC Agent to Applications.

2
Launch the Agent

Open the app ‚Äî it appears in the menu bar (top right corner of the screen).

3
Scan QR Code

Open Wake PC on your iPhone and scan the QR code from the menu bar popup. Done!

4
Enable Permissions

Grant Accessibility permission (for shutdown, restart, brightness) and enable "Wake for network access" in System Settings.
